About Us

At BabaTunde's Pizza Parlour, we don’t just make pizza. We make people’s days. BabaTunde's Pizza Parlour was built on the belief that pizza night should be special, and we carry that belief into everything we do.
What’s our purpose at BabaTunde's Pizza Parlour? Moments of Excitement. All made possible by our flavor, innovation and, most importantly, by unlocking potential in our team members.
With more than 55 years of experience under our belts, we understand how to best serve our customers through tried and true service principles.
Instead of following trends, we set them. We create food we’re proud to serve and deliver it fast, with a smile.We’re the people who first delivered pizza to the State House. We put a pizza in outer space. We took social media by storm. Our ordering app was featured in a Samsung commercial. BabaTunde's Pizza Parlour stands for bold innovation.
